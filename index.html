About Me

My name is Victoire and I am a full-stack developer.

I decided to do a career change the last year, and for 6 months I learned coding with online courses as a self-taught.

I began to learn HTML, CSS, Javascript and JQuery without any formal education in computer science, only via the websites like codecademy, freecodecamp, openclassroom, w3schools…

I completed this learning process by the Coding Boot Camp at UC Berkeley extension. It was a really great experience where I learned a lot.

During this program, I attended informative lectures and took part in a variety of individual and team exercises, working independently and in groups, in the classroom and at home.

To build my projects, I had to combine my knowledges of frond-end and back-end technologies. You will find my portfolio which recounts what I could accomplish during my apprenticeship.
